Cookies

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and login details. Cookies can be either "persistent" (stored for a set period) or "session" (deleted when you close your browser).

What Cookies Do We Use?

Rolnopol uses the following types of cookies:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
rolnopolToken Authentication token for user login sessions Session or as configured
rolnopolIsLogged Indicates whether the user is logged in Session or as configured
rolnopolUsername Stores the username for personalization Session or as configured
rolnopolCookieConsent Records your cookie consent preference 7 days
rolnopolPromoAdvertSeen Tracks promotional popup display frequency Session

Your Cookie Choices

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through browser settings. You can typically:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Decline cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Receive a notification when a website tries to place a cookie

Please note that declining cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of Rolnopol, such as staying logged in or personalizing your experience.
